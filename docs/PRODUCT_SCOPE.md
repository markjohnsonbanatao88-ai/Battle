# Product Scope

The complete product scope is defined in:

- `00_MASTER_BUILD_BLUEPRINT.md`
- `01_PRODUCT_REQUIREMENTS.md`
- `03_WORKFLOWS.md`
- `15_IMPLEMENTATION_ROADMAP.md`

The product is a public law-firm website plus private law-office operating system covering intake, conflict checks, contacts, matters, deadlines, documents, client collaboration, billing, governance and controlled AI.

No public credentials, practice claims, fees, results or testimonials may be published until recorded as approved in `VERIFIED_CONTENT_REGISTER.md` and `19_DECISION_REGISTER.md`.
