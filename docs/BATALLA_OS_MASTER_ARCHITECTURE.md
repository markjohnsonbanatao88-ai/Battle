# BatallaOS Master Architecture

This is the canonical product mandate for BatallaOS.

The complete authoritative architecture is maintained in [`27_BATALLA_OS_MASTER_ARCHITECTURE.md`](27_BATALLA_OS_MASTER_ARCHITECTURE.md).

BatallaOS is the complete digital operating system for Batalla & Associates. It is not merely a CRM, public website, dashboard, or AI chatbot.

The system is centered on the Case Management Core and must preserve:

- all 23 core modules;
- physical and digital folder synchronization;
- old-school, print-first usability;
- role-specific experiences for Atty. Batalla, Dan Tejada, and the secretary;
- strict legal approval controls;
- controlled AI that assists but never files, sends, approves, deletes, or replaces lawyer judgment;
- the six-phase implementation roadmap;
- the Executive Command Center;
- end-to-end legal-safety, authorization, audit, testing, and rollback requirements.

No implementation epic may redefine or reduce this scope. Individual epics are subordinate slices of this operating system.
